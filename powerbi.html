Power BI Internship Training

Learn Data Analytics, Visualization & Business Intelligence with Real-Time Projects

About Power BI Internship

Power BI Internship at TechnoCovai is designed for students who want to build strong skills in data analytics and business intelligence. This program covers complete data workflow including data cleaning, data modeling, DAX, visualization, and advanced Power BI concepts.

Data Cleaning (Power Query)

  • Import data from Excel, CSV, SQL
  • Remove duplicates & errors
  • Handle missing values
  • Data transformation & formatting
  • Merge and append queries

Data Modeling

  • Relationships (One-to-Many, Many-to-Many)
  • Star Schema & Snowflake Schema
  • Primary & Foreign keys
  • Normalization basics
  • Optimizing data models

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

  • Calculated Columns & Measures
  • SUM, COUNT, AVERAGE
  • CALCULATE function
  • Time Intelligence (YTD, MTD)
  • Conditional formulas

Data Visualization

  • Bar, Line, Pie, Donut Charts
  • Tables & Matrix
  • Cards & KPIs
  • Slicers & Filters
  • Dashboard Design

Advanced Power BI Concepts

  • Drill-down & Drill-through
  • Bookmarks & Navigation
  • Row Level Security (RLS)
  • Power BI Service (Publish & Share)
  • Performance optimization
